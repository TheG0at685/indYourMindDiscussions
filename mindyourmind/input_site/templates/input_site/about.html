mindyourmind exists in the space where mental health, wellness, engagement and technology meet. We work with young people aged 14 to 29 to co-create interactive tools and innovative resources to build capacity and resilience.



Nearly half of all youth have suffered from some sort of mental illness in their lives. This trend is only growing so it is we strive to help reverse this problem.



mindyourmind discussions is a platform that enables users to discuss issues and possible solutions in an open setting. To contribute to the conversation, create an account.
